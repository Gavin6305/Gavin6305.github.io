Hello, my name is Gavin Pinto. This is my course portfolio.

This portfolio contains various audio works and reports that share one common theme, which is sound, when truly listened to, can be treated as an experience.

Digital Audio Projects

  • Digital Audio Project 1

    This audio work mainly consists of recorded sounds that are loosely associated with the theme of starting a new day and partaking in a minor adventure.

  • Digital Audio Project 2

    DAP 2 takes sounds from DAP 1 and incorporates rhythm by modifying the sounds to act as instruments, forming a new sound entirely.

  • Digital Audio Project 3

    The final audio project uses sounds from DAP 1 and introduces a theme that is left to interpretation. It incorporates rhythm, tone, and audio effects/instruments to enhance the mood that is felt by the listener.
